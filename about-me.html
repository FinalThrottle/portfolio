About Me

So who is Alex Lam?

Hello! If you've somehow stumbled upon my portfolio, you must also be curious to know who I am. My name is Alex, and I am an alumni of the University of Washington, graduated in 2015. At first, I was in pursuit of a career in the medical field. However, after working in the inpatient setting, it turns out that it was something that I did not want to pursue afterall.

As a kid, I somewhat dabbled into the world of coding. I was obsessed with the idea of creating a website, and making it look as stunning as possible - it evolved into a fan site for a video game. However, I faced an obstacle. There were limitations of what 12 year old me could do. I just didn't understand anything beyond HTML, and because of that, I abandoned the world of creating visually stunning websites. So here I am again, 13 years later, diving into the world of coding and creating websites once more.
